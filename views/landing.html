What is Nirdizati?

Nirdizati is a dashboard-based monitoring tool, which is updated periodically based on incoming streams of events.

However, unlike classical monitoring dashboards, Nirdizati does not only focus on showing the current state of business process executions, but also, and more importantly, it sheds lights on their future state. “How long will it take to handle this claim?, Will this loan offer be accepted?” are typical questions Nirdizati can answer, quite accurately.

What can I do with Nirdizati?
1
Monitor how your processes behave in real-time
  • How many cases are running now?
  • How long does it take on average per case?
  • How many events have elapsed so far?
2
Nail down potential performance issues
  • When will this case be finished?
  • How likely is this case will be accepted?
  • What are the most likely future activites in this case?
3
Check how your current processes stack up against their past executions
  • Are current processes slower than they used to be?
  • Are current processes less error-prone?
